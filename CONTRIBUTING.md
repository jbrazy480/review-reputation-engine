# Contributing

Thanks for helping build the best open review and reputation system. Pull requests welcome: add a vertical, sharpen the copy, or improve the tool.

Maintained by **James Hill ("The AI Guy")**, founder of **[RizzDial](https://rizzdial.com)**. More at **[aiguyofficial.com](https://aiguyofficial.com)**.

## Ground rules

- **Follow the framework.** Every system uses the parts in [docs/REVIEW-FRAMEWORK.md](docs/REVIEW-FRAMEWORK.md): The Ask, The Sequence, The Messages, Smart Routing, the Response Library, and the Build Spec.
- **Ask everyone, suppress nothing.** Happy customers get a one-tap Google link. Unhappy customers get a private path first. Never fake, pay for, or hide reviews. Anything that selectively suppresses negative public reviews is out.
- **Truthful and brand safe.** RizzDial is a proprietary AI sales platform. No fake numbers, no invented prices, no fake reviews. GoHighLevel is one optional CRM, not a foundation.
- **No em dashes or en dashes.** House style. Use periods, commas, and parentheses.
- **Copy rules.** Reference the specific job. One-tap public link. Reply to every review, fast, calm, human, never argue, never reveal private details.

## The fastest way to contribute

Build it with `python3 generate.py`, then refine the output. The tool already emits the correct structure, so your contribution stays consistent.

## Review

A maintainer reviews for the framework, the honest review rule, truthfulness, the no em dash rule, and copy quality. Once it passes, it gets merged. Thank you.
